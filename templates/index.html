Movies

The dataset consists of over 5,000 movies released before 2017. Data points include countries, movie directors, budget, revenue, release dates, languages, production companies, IMDB vote counts and vote averages.

The areas in red on the map are the film locations. The map is interactive. You can click on the country to filter the data according to location.

Row chart representing all movie directors. The first line includes all producers and the next 4 represent the top 4 movie directors based on how many movies they've produced.
As you scroll over, the total number of movies will appear accordingly.
Pie chart representing all countries movies have been produced. The top 3 country with the highest number of movies are displayed along with a section called "others" which represents all the other countries.
As you scroll over the chart, the total number of movies will display accordingly.
Bar chart representing the budget spent per movie from 0-100million+. The amount is split into 7 parts to show the difference in number of movies created according to the budget
As you scroll over left and right, the total number of movies will display accordingly.
